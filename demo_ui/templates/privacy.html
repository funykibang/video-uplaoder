TikTok Video Scheduler

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2025

project-dythq.vercel.app ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our video scheduling service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok account data — access tokens obtained via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, used solely to post content on your behalf.
  • Video files — files you upload are stored temporarily in memory while being processed and posted to TikTok. They are deleted immediately after the upload completes.
  • Post metadata — titles, captions, and scheduled times you provide when creating a post.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via the official TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Upload and publish videos to your TikTok account at your direction.
  • Schedule posts for future publication as configured by you.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or TikTok credentials with any third party.

3. TikTok API Data

This application uses the TikTok Content Posting API. By connecting your TikTok account, you agree to TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We only request the minimum permissions required to post content and never access your messages, followers, or other private data.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Video files are stored in temporary memory during upload and deleted immediately upon completion or failure.
  • OAuth access tokens are stored securely as environment variables on the server and are never logged or exposed.
  • We do not maintain a database of your posts or account activity beyond what is visible in the application's session.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted HTTPS connections and secure credential storage. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service communicates exclusively with TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com). We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or any other third-party data processors.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app management settings. Upon revocation, all stored tokens become invalid and are discarded.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
